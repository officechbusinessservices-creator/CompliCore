## Lead Qualification Criteria

### Keep:
- 50–200 units
- 1–3 leasing staff
- Manual inquiry handling visible (slow response on listings)
- No automation stack in job postings or website copy

### Remove:
- Large enterprise operators
- Under 20 units
- Already using RealPage, Yardi automation modules
- Not in leasing-active markets

---

## Demo Script (15 minutes)

**Minutes 1–2 — Problem confirmation**
> "Walk me through what happens when a new inquiry comes in today."
> Listen for: delay, manual steps, missed follow-ups.

**Minutes 3–6 — Simulation**
> Run live demo on /demo
> Show: inquiry → response → follow-up → routing

**Minutes 7–9 — Gap**
> "Your current system does not do this automatically."
> Let them confirm.

**Minutes 10–12 — Solution**
> "CompliCore+ installs this. Activation takes one session after payment."

**Minutes 13–15 — Close**
> "We activate this now and start with one flow. $1,500 activation + $349/month."
> Send Stripe checkout link immediately if they say yes.
