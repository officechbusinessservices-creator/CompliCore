# 10. Agentic OS Doctrine (2026–2030)

## 10.1 Mission Shift

Complicore evolves from a sovereign RWA ledger into a **Sovereign Agentic Operating System** for global travel and mobility.

- **RWA Core** remains the legal-financial ground truth.
- **Agentic Mesh** becomes the execution layer for autonomous intent.
- **Policy-as-Code + Liability Wrappers** enforce deterministic safety.

---

## 10.2 Strategic Convergence

### Core thesis

1. Ledger-grade finality is prerequisite for safe AI autonomy.
2. Agentic systems require deterministic compliance at decision time.
3. Sovereign identity + atomic settlement creates defensible moat.

### Outcome

Complicore shifts from inventory aggregation to **liability orchestration**:

- Agent-to-agent (A2A) commerce can negotiate and settle in real time.
- Every autonomous action is policy-scored and traceable.
- CFO-grade compliance artifacts are generated by design.

---

## 10.3 Non-Defeatable Pillars

### A) Sovereign Journey Orchestration

Target corridor: **Singapore ↔ Dubai**.

- Identity bridge: SSI + national/eID frameworks.
- Travel flow: intent → planning agents → compliance gate → settlement finality.
- Biometric-ready posture with ZK-compatible verification chain.

### B) Compliance-as-a-Moat

- Carbon and policy budgets become executable governance rules.
- Scope-3/event traces become immutable evidence.
- AI decisions are wrapped by deterministic constraints, not post-facto audits.

### C) A2A Commerce

- Budget/Lodging/Flight agents negotiate continuously under policy guardrails.
- Instant settlement rails can unlock negotiated discounts.
- Human escalation is enforced below confidence thresholds.

---

## 10.4 Foundational Operating Rules

1. **No autonomous financial action without policy wrapper pass.**
2. **No sensitive action without identity assertion context.**
3. **Confidence below threshold mandates Human-in-the-Loop (HITL).**
4. **Every agent decision must produce an auditable trail.**

---

---

## 10.6 Settlement + Agent Constitution Binding

Complicore now includes two machine-readable foundations for autonomous commerce:

- **Settlement Contract Template:** `contracts/ComplicoreInstantSettlement.sol`
  - Escrow-at-procurement
  - Oracle-gated biometric/identity settlement release
  - Controlled cancellation/refund path pre-settlement
- **Sub-Agent Constitution:** `AGENTS.md`
  - Deterministic fiscal/carbon/privacy guardrails
  - Workflow contract for Singpass/UAE Pass, friction scoring, and post-check-in credential delivery
  - Violation protocol requiring HITL override on CSRD/Scope-3 risk

Together these bind policy intent (agent behavior) with programmable settlement finality (contract execution).
